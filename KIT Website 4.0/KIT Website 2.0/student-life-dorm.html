Dorm & Housing

On-campus living, with 24/7 security.

The KIT dormitory on Koh Pich puts students minutes from classrooms, labs, and the library — with furnished rooms, utilities, Wi-Fi, laundry, optional meal plans, and 24/7 on-site security. Live where you study, without the friction of a daily commute.

Room types

Three room types, one secure building.

Private

Single room

A private furnished room with a single bed, study desk, wardrobe, and Wi-Fi — ideal for students who want quiet, focused space.

  • Bed, desk, wardrobe, chair
  • Private en-suite or shared floor bath
  • Wi-Fi & utilities included
Shared

Shared twin

A furnished twin room shared with one other student — the most affordable on-campus option, with the same access to all dorm facilities.

  • Two beds, two desks, two wardrobes
  • Shared floor bathroom & kitchen
  • Wi-Fi & utilities included
Premium

Studio

A self-contained studio with private bathroom and a small kitchenette — for students who prefer independent living with on-campus security.

  • Private bath & kitchenette
  • Larger desk & storage
  • Wi-Fi & utilities included
What's included

Everything you need, in one fee.

Furniture

Furnished rooms

Every room comes furnished — bed, study desk, chair, and wardrobe. Studios add a kitchenette; shared twins include two of each. No need to buy or move furniture.

Utilities

Utilities included

Electricity, water, and air-conditioning are included in the housing fee — so there's no separate utility bill to manage each month.

Connectivity

Campus Wi-Fi

High-speed Wi-Fi across the dormitory — on a separate residential network from the academic buildings, with wired access available in study areas.

Services

Laundry

Shared laundry facilities on dorm floors — with washers and dryers available at no extra cost for residents. Bedding is provided at check-in.

Dining

Meal plan

Optional meal plans at the on-campus cafeteria — with breakfast, lunch, and dinner options, and a mix of Cambodian and international dishes.

See cafeteria →
Safety

24/7 Security

On-site security staffed around the clock, card-controlled building access, and CCTV in common areas — so residents and their families can rest easy.

How to apply for housing

Four steps from offer to move-in.

01

Get admitted

Receive and accept your KIT admission offer. Housing applications open once you're admitted to a program.

02

Apply for housing

Submit the housing application form, ranking your preferred room types. Availability is allocated on a first-come basis.

03

Pay the deposit

Confirm your room with a housing deposit. The deposit secures your room type for the upcoming term or academic year.

04

Move in

Arrive on move-in day, collect your access card and bedding, and settle in. Orientation for new residents runs the same week.

FAQ

Common questions about dorm life at KIT.

Is on-campus housing guaranteed for new students?

Housing is allocated on a first-come basis after admission. We strongly recommend submitting your housing application and deposit as soon as you accept your admission offer. Most admitted students who apply promptly receive a room on campus.

Can I choose my roommate in a shared twin?

Yes. If you and another admitted student both request each other as roommates on your housing applications, we'll do our best to place you together. Otherwise, roommates are matched by program and lifestyle preferences where possible.
