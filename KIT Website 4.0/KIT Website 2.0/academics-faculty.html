How we teach

Faculty who build the curricula and mentor the cohort.

Every program is taught by a mix of senior academic faculty and industry practitioners — in English, in small cohorts, with regular office hours and project mentorship. Faculty also serve as internship supervisors, working with partner employers in Cambodia and Japan to review student work and provide feedback.

Curricula are reviewed with industry partners each year, and faculty are evaluated on both classroom teaching and the placement outcomes of the students they mentor — not just on coursework delivered.
