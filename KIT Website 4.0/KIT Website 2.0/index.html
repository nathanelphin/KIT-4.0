General Information

An English-medium university built around one outcome: a global career.

Kirirom Institute of Technology (KIT) is an English-medium university on Koh Pich, Phnom Penh. Programs are taught in small cohorts by faculty drawn from industry and academia, with curricula reviewed against real employer requirements — and a formal pathway that places qualified graduates into careers in Japan.
