Access & support

Open resources, available when you need them.

Every resource above is included for enrolled students — no separate fees. The library, computer labs and AI & Robotics Lab keep open access hours throughout each teaching term, and career services runs weekly drop-in sessions for internship and Japan-pathway preparation.

Learning support & tutoring is built around faculty office hours and peer study groups, so students always have a clear next step when a course gets hard — and the Japanese Language Center runs structured tracks aligned to the Career in Japan pathway timeline.
