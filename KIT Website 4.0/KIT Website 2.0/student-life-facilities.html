Campus Facilities

Modern facilities built for making and learning.

Everything KIT students need — classrooms, an AI & robotics lab, computer labs, library and study spaces, cafeteria, and recreation areas — on a single campus on Koh Pich, Diamond Island, Phnom Penh. Labs and the library stay open late; security is on-site 24/7.

What's on campus

Six core facilities, one short walk.

Learning

Classrooms

English-medium classrooms sized for small cohorts — with projectors, whiteboards, and seating arranged for discussion rather than lecture-only delivery.

Lab

AI & Robotics Lab

A new lab space for hands-on AI, computer vision, and robotics coursework — with workstations, sensor kits, and robot platforms for student projects.

See in-house projects →
Study

Library & Study Spaces

Quiet study areas, group-study rooms, and a curated collection supporting all six programs — with Wi-Fi, power, and late-opening hours on most weekdays.

Lab

Computer Labs

Spec'd for software engineering and AI & ML coursework — with the IDEs, ML frameworks, and design tools students need, plus GPU access for ML workloads.

Dining

Cafeteria

An on-campus cafeteria serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner — with meal-plan options for dorm residents and a mix of Cambodian and international dishes.

Dorm meal plans →
Wellness

Sports & Recreation

Recreation space for student clubs, fitness, and downtime — supporting a healthy campus rhythm between classes, labs, and internships.

Access & security

When you can use the facilities.

Open late, secured always.

The library and labs keep extended hours so students can work around class, internship, and Japanese-language schedules. Building access is controlled and the campus has 24/7 on-site security — especially important for dorm residents.

Specific access varies by facility and term — students receive an access card at enrollment, and lab access for after-hours work is granted through the relevant course or project supervisor.

Equipment & software

Tools students actually use.

Hardware

Workstations & lab equipment

Modern computer-lab workstations, GPU-equipped machines for ML workloads, robotics platforms and sensor kits in the AI & Robotics Lab, and AV equipment for design and architecture coursework.

Software

IDEs, ML frameworks & design tools

Standard software engineering IDEs, ML and computer-vision frameworks, design and architecture tools (CAD, UX, visual design), and Japanese-language learning platforms — available on lab machines and via student licenses.

Connectivity

Campus Wi-Fi & network

High-speed Wi-Fi across classrooms, labs, library, cafeteria, and dormitory — with separate secure networks for academic and residential use, and wired access in computer labs.

Support

IT & lab support

On-site IT support during business hours, with lab supervisors available during scheduled sessions and project work — so students can resolve issues without losing a study session.
