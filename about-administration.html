Founder / Administration / Chairman

Leadership guided by industry and education.

KIT is led by a founder and chairman with a background in both industry and education, supported by a president, vice-presidents, deans and an advisory structure that keeps every academic decision tied to employer outcomes.

Messages from leadership

In their own words.

The direction of KIT is set by its Chairman and carried out by its President. Read their full messages below — the vision behind the institute, and the academic leadership that turns it into daily practice.

Message from the Chairman

Chairman

Welcome to Kirirom Institute of Technology (KIT) — a place where innovation meets opportunity, and where young minds are nurtured to become global leaders in technology and innovation.

As the Chairman of KIT, my vision is to transform this institute into a world-class hub for Artificial Intelligence, Generative AI, and advanced engineering education — alongside Tourism and Hotel Management and Design Architecture as our core pillars. In a world where technology evolves every second, it is our mission to prepare students not just for the jobs of today, but for the opportunities and challenges of tomorrow.

At KIT, our commitment goes beyond academics. With Japanese management principles at the core of our operations, we emphasize discipline, precision, and a strong work ethic — qualities that ensure our graduates stand out in competitive global markets. Our students benefit from major placement opportunities in Japan, a testament to the quality of education, mentorship, and practical exposure they receive here.

We are building an ecosystem that integrates academic rigor, hands-on training, and innovation-driven research. Our partnerships with industry leaders in Japan and across Southeast Asia allow our students to gain real-world experience, bridging the gap between education and employability.

KIT is more than just an institute; it is a platform for dreams and ideas. Whether it’s through state-of-the-art AI labs, an industry-aligned curriculum, or our nurturing campus environment in the serene beauty of Kirirom National Park, every element here is designed to inspire excellence.

As we move forward, our goal is to position KIT as a regional leader in technology education, empowering our graduates to become innovators, entrepreneurs, and change-makers — not just in Asia, but globally.

I invite you to be a part of this exciting journey — as a student, partner, or supporter — and help us shape the future of technology together.

KC

Kiran Churakanti

Chairman, Kirirom Institute of Technology (KIT)

President

President

Leo Fernandez brings over 23 years of extensive experience in Information Technology across both industry and academia, with a strong foundation in academic leadership, institutional development, and technology-driven education.

His professional career began in the software industry, where he served for five years as a Senior Software Programmer, contributing to high-profile projects and gaining valuable industry expertise in software development and project execution. He later expanded his professional journey into entrepreneurship, innovation, and organizational leadership — experiences that strengthened his strategic thinking and managerial capabilities.

Further enriching his professional profile, Leo served for three years as a Professional Software Trainer and held a managerial position at a prestigious Software Training Center, where he focused on enhancing operational efficiency, developing industry-oriented training programs, and fostering effective learning environments.

Transitioning into academia, Leo has dedicated the past fourteen years to higher education and academic excellence. His academic journey includes serving at a well-known university in Tanzania, followed by twelve years of distinguished service at reputed higher education institutions in Cambodia. During this period, he progressed through several leadership roles, from IT Lecturer to Vice President, demonstrating a consistent commitment to academic quality, faculty development, student success, and institutional advancement.

Throughout his career, Leo has actively contributed to curriculum innovation, outcome-based education, technology integration in teaching and learning, strategic academic planning, and institutional leadership. His diverse blend of industry experience and academic insight enables him to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, preparing students to meet the evolving demands of the global technology landscape.

Driven by a passion for lifelong learning and educational transformation, Leo continues to advocate for innovation, academic excellence, and student-centered learning in higher education.

LF

Leo Fernandez

President, Kirirom Institute of Technology (KIT)

Governance

How decisions get made — and checked.

KIT's governance is built on two ideas: academic quality is a faculty responsibility, and academic quality is meaningless without employer signal. Four bodies keep both sides honest.

Academic Council

Curriculum, assessment & faculty

Chaired by the VP for Academic Affairs and including the deans and elected faculty representatives. Approves new programs, reviews assessment standards, and signs off on faculty appointments and promotions.

Industry Advisory Board

Employer signal on every program

Senior representatives from Cambodian and Japanese employer partners. Meets twice a year to review curriculum against hiring requirements and to nominate internship and placement opportunities for students.

Quality Assurance

Outcomes & accreditation

An internal QA office reports to the president and tracks placement rates, employer satisfaction surveys, and accreditation compliance with Cambodian higher-education regulators.

Partnerships

Employers, universities & Japan network

A dedicated function within the president's office manages employer partnerships, university exchange agreements, and the Japan-side relationships that feed the Career in Japan pathway.
