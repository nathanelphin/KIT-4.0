Visit Us

Come see KIT for yourself.

There's no better way to understand KIT than to walk through the doors. Our Koh Pich campus sits on Diamond Island in central Phnom Penh — book a guided tour, join an open day, or take a virtual walkthrough from anywhere.

What to expect

A real look at student life — not a sales pitch.

Our tours are led by current students, not recruiters. You'll see classrooms during real teaching hours, walk through the dorm and cafeteria, and have time to ask anything — about classes, scholarships, the Career in Japan pathway, or what living on Koh Pich is actually like.

Bring your family — parents are welcome, and tours are available in both English and Khmer.

How to find us

On Koh Pich — Diamond Island, Phnom Penh.

The KIT campus sits on Koh Pich (Diamond Island), in the Chamkar Mon district of Phnom Penh. It's a 10-minute tuk-tuk from Aeon Mall 1 and roughly 25 minutes from Phnom Penh International Airport outside peak hours.

Free visitor parking is available on campus. The admissions office is on the ground floor — ask for the visit desk on arrival.
