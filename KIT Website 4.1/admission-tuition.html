Payment options

Pay in a way that fits your family's cash flow.

Tuition can be paid per term or per year. Per-term payments split annual tuition into two equal installments, due before the start of each term. Annual payment receives a small early-settlement discount.

Payments are made by bank transfer to the KIT accounts team. Need to spread payments further? An installment plan is available for families who need monthly billing — speak with the admissions team to set it up.

FAQ

Common questions about tuition.

When is tuition due?

Tuition is due before the start of each term. For per-term payers, that means two installments per year — one in August before Term 1 and one in January before Term 2. Annual payers settle the full year in August. Late payment may incur a small administrative fee and affect enrollment.

Are there payment plans available?

Yes. Families can request a monthly installment plan that spreads annual tuition across the academic year. There is no interest charge on installment plans, but an administrative fee may apply. Contact the admissions team before enrollment to set up a plan that fits your situation.

Worried about cost?

See what aid you qualify for.

Merit scholarships of 10–25% and need-based aid can take a meaningful bite out of tuition. Start your application and we'll assess your eligibility together.
