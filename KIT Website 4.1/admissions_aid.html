Why apply to KIT

English-medium. Industry-aligned. Built for outcomes.

KIT keeps admission straightforward so Cambodian students can focus on what matters — building the skills that employers in Cambodia and Japan actually need. No IELTS or TOEFL barrier, transparent tuition posted publicly, and merit scholarships of 10–25% that reward strong academic work.

Every application is read by the admissions team. Qualified applicants are invited to a short interview — in person at our Koh Pich campus or online — and offers typically follow within days.
