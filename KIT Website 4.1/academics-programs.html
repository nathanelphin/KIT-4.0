Which program is right for you?

A few of the questions we hear most often from prospective students — open each one for a short answer.

Can I switch programs after my first year?

Yes. The first year of every program shares common foundations in programming, mathematics and English-medium communication, which makes it possible to transfer between programs at the start of the second year with faculty approval.

Do all six programs feed into the Career in Japan pathway?

Yes. Every program includes structured Japanese language support and the Career in Japan pathway is open to qualified graduates across all six programs. Placement depends on language readiness, technical portfolio and partner employer demand in your field.
